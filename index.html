Who Are We

Delta MegaBricks is a corporate institute with 20 partners saddled with the responsibility of building safe haven with recycled plastic. It is founded in 2022 and can give futuristic outfit with projection 2022-2030

The Vision

Do you know that there is an excessive use of plastic (around 2600 tonnes per day) in Nigeria? Because of this, our mother nature is getting affected severely. There is, therefore, a need to reduce this waste. Our vision is to produce bricks, which are majorly made out of plastics.

We are focused on producing high quality products that will lead to a sustainable green environment and will create maximum value addition for our customers

Our Products

PET Flakes

This plastic is reclaimed from post-consumer soft drink bottles and returned to the manufacturing process for use in carpet, bottles and strapping

HDPE Flakes

This plastic is reclaimed from milk containers and returned to the manufacturing process for use in bottles, shipping bags and containers.

Recycling Is Important

Our community, nation and the world at large are the ultimate benefactors.

It's easy to see why recycling plastic is so important. By recycling, we can help conserve the additional 80% of the energy that is typically used when making new plastic bottles, containers and other items.

For every 1 ton of plastic that is recycled, reports estimate that 7 yards of landfill space are saved.
